About Carlton Leisure

Carlton Leisure is one of the leading travel agents in London, UK offering cheap flights to Bangkok, Sydney, Melbourne, New Delhi, Mumbai, Brisbane, Sao Paulo and other parts of the world. You can easily book cheap flights to Dubai, New York, Johannesburg, Auckland, Perth, Manila, Kuala Lumpur, Singapore, Hong Kong, Cape Town, Shanghai, Rio De Janeiro, Washington and other top vacation destinations of the world. We also offer City Break, Short Break, Hotels and Package Holidays to worldwide destinations. We work with Emirates, Qatar Airways, Etihad Airways, TAM Airlines, Malaysia Airlines, British Airways, Virgin Atlantic, Iberia Airlines, Jet Airways, Thai Airways, Turkish Airlines, Eva Airways, Gulf Air, Tap Portugal, Kuwait Airways, South African Airways, Brussels Airlines, Oman Air, Vietnam Airlines and many other top Airlines in the world. All the flights you book with us are ATOL and IATA protected allowing you to book with confidence.
